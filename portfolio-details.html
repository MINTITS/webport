Empowering a Local Manufacturer with Data & Digital Workflows

The Challenge: A well-established Massachusetts-based manufacturing company faced operational inefficiencies due to outdated manual processes, disparate data sources, and a lack of real-time visibility into key performance indicators (KPIs). This impacted their ability to make timely decisions, optimize production, and scale effectively.

The MINT ITS Solution: Our team partnered closely with the client to develop and implement a comprehensive digital transformation and business intelligence strategy. This involved:

  • Automating core workflows by implementing cloud-based productivity tools (Microsoft 365) and integrating AI-driven processes within a tailored CRM system for better sales and customer data management.
  • Developing custom Business Intelligence dashboards using Power BI to consolidate data from production, sales, and inventory, providing actionable insights at a glance.
  • Streamlining document management and collaboration through secure cloud storage solutions.
  • Providing ongoing Managed IT services to ensure the reliability and security of the new digital infrastructure, including proactive monitoring and helpdesk support.

Key Outcomes:

  • Significant improvement in operational efficiency and reduction in manual data entry.
  • Enhanced decision-making capabilities through access to real-time, accurate data.
  • Improved collaboration across departments with centralized information.
  • A scalable technology foundation to support future growth and innovation.
  • Empowered leadership with clear insights into their business landscape, leading to more confident strategic planning.
